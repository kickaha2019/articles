Title:
	Issues on OSX 10.9 (Mavericks)

Text:
	[Wasteland 2] failed to
	start, ''syspolicyd'' demon went to 40% CPU busy continuously,
	message about verifying.

Code:
	Issue with Gatekeeper verifying the application. Manually
	unquarantining [Wasteland 2] with ''xattr -d -r com.apple.quarantine''
	got the application to start.

Text:
	''Apple Mail'' suddenly lost the ''Drafts'', ''Junk'', and
	''Trash'' mailboxes in the list of mailboxes.

Code:
	Restarting the Mac got them back.
