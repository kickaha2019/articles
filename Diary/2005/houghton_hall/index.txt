Title:
	Houghton Hall

Anchor:
  Houghton Hall

Date:
	Monday, 29 August 2005

Text:
	The seat of Robert Walpole Britain's first prime minister, later associated with the Cholmondeley family (pronounced Chumley.) Fine set of rooms but not a coherent style. Good gardens.
