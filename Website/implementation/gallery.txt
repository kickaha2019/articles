Text:
	The "Gallery" declaration adds a table of images to the article 
	page.
	
	Example:
	
Code:
	Gallery:
		coventry_cobweb.JPG
		Cobweb in Coventry
	
		pooles_cavern_1.JPG
		Poole's Cavern

		pooles_cavern_2.JPG
		Poole's Cavern
	
		crich_1.JPG
		Crich Tramway Museum

		crich_2.JPG
		Crich Tramway Museum
		
Text:
	Which gives:
	
Gallery:
	coventry_cobweb.JPG
	Cobweb in Coventry
	
	pooles_cavern_1.JPG
	Poole's Cavern

	pooles_cavern_2.JPG
	Poole's Cavern
	
	crich_1.JPG
	Crich Tramway Museum

	crich_2.JPG
	Crich Tramway Museum
	
Text:
	The dimensions of the images in the gallery are controlled by parameters
	"GALLERY_HEIGHT" and "GALLERY_WIDTH".  The images are scaled to fit into these
	dimensions while preserving the aspect ratio.
